Smoothie

Description

A strawberry smoothie is a refreshing and healthy drink made by blending together fresh or frozen strawberries, milk, yogurt, and ice. It has a bright pink color and a sweet, fruity flavor that is perfect for a quick breakfast or afternoon pick-me-up. You can customize your smoothie by adding other ingredients such as bananas, spinach, or protein powder to give it a boost of nutrition. Enjoy your strawberry smoothie chilled, garnished with a few extra strawberries on top.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vanilla yogurt
  • 1/2 cup ice
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)

Steps

  1. Place the frozen strawberries, milk, yogurt, ice, and honey (if using) in a blender.
  2. Blend on high speed until the smoothie is thick and creamy, about 1-2 minutes.
  3. Taste and adjust sweetness as desired. You can add more honey or a few drops of liquid sweetener if needed.
  4. Pour the smoothie into a tall glass and serve immediately.
