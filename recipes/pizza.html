Pizza

Description

A pizza is a savory dish made up of a baked, round bread crust topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of meats, vegetables, and spices. The crust can be thin and crispy, or thick and chewy, and is usually topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings such as sausage, pepperoni, mushroom, onion, and bell peppers.

Pizza is a popular food around the world and is often eaten as a snack, appetizer, or main dish. It can be served hot or cold, and is a common food at parties, picnics, and other gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the pizza dough into a 12-inch circle.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza pan.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edge.
  5. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Arrange the pepperoni slices on top of the cheese.
  7. Sprinkle the parmesan cheese over the pepperoni.
  8. Bake the pizza for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove the pizza from the oven and sprinkle with chopped fresh basil. Cut into slices and serve hot.
